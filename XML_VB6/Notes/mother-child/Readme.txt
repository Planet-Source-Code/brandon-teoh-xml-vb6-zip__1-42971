This sample demonstrate mother and child relationship of a xml document.

nodeType values:

 1 = NODE_ELEMENT  
 2 = NODE_ATTRIBUTE 
 3 = NODE_TEXT
 4 = NODE_CDATA_SECTION
 5 = NODE_ENTITY_REFERENCE
 6 = NODE_ENTITY 
 7 = NODE_PROCESSING_INSTRUCTION
 8 = NODE_COMMENT 
 9 = NODE_DOCUMENT 
 10 = NODE_DOCUMENT_TYPE
 11 = NODE_DOCUMENT_FRAGMENT
 12 = NODE_NOTATION 

What you need ?
----------------
1. MSXML4 (http://download.microsoft.com/download/xml/SP/40SP1/WIN98MeXP/EN-US/msxml.msi)
2. VB6 with at least SP2 (for TreeView control) 

Procedure
---------
1. Install MSXML4 first
2. Run the program.

conclusion
----------
The final child is the tag value.
